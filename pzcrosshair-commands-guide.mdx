<Badge>Player Commands</Badge>

<Note>
No admin permissions are required. These commands are available to all players by default.
</Note>

<Card title="/crosshair — Open Main Menu" icon="crosshairs">
Opens the primary interface where players can toggle their crosshair, access the native/image editors, manage profiles, and import/export share codes.
</Card>

<Card title="/aim — Quick Alias" icon="bullseye">
A quick alias that performs the exact same function as /crosshair for ease of use.
</Card>
