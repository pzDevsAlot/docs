Whether you are looking for advanced utility scripts like `pz_discord` or immersive gameplay features like `pz_pawnshop`, our resources are built with performance and developer flexibility in mind.

## Getting Started

To get the most out of pz_Dev resources, we recommend following our structured documentation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Global Requirements">
    Ensure your server artifacts are up to date and you have the necessary dependencies installed (e.g., `ox_lib` or `qb-core`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Installation">
    Follow the specific guide for your purchased resource to ensure correct SQL injection and file placement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configuration">
    Modify the `config.lua` to match your server's economy and permission levels.
  </Step>
</Steps>
